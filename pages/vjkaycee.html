VJ KayCee

KCee is a visual artist, creative director, technologist, and the founder of Pixadoo Visuals Pvt. Ltd., one of India’s leading names in projection mapping, immersive experiences, concert visuals, and experience-led storytelling. With over 20 years of experience in content design and live visual culture, he began his journey as a VJ at a time when visual jockeying was still an emerging language in India, and went on to help shape the way live events, concerts, brand experiences, immersive experiences, and public installations use visuals as a central storytelling force.

What sets KCee apart is his rare balance of creative vision and executional depth. He is not only someone who imagines large-scale visual experiences, but also someone who understands how to build them, adapt them, and make them work in the real world. Across Pixadoo’s body of work, he has consistently pushed the boundaries of what’s possible. He has redefined what LEDs, lights, screens, projection surfaces, and live visuals are meant to do, or even capable of becoming. He is always discovering or creating new ways to use technology beyond its conventional purpose, often turning technical systems into immersive environments.

Under his creative leadership, Pixadoo has delivered landmark work across concerts, music festivals, luxury private celebrations, projection mapping, government showcases, brand launches, and immersive installations. In each project, KCee has remained personally involved in the creative direction, experience curation, visual design, and technical thinking, ensuring that every production uses technology not just as a tool, but as a medium for emotion, scale, storytelling, and immersive experience design.

His love for technology and his hands-on understanding of the challenges faced by content creators led to the creation of PIXMAP, a professional pixel-mapping tool built for stage, LED, and live-event workflows. Developed from years of real-world experience across complex productions, PIXMAP is KCee’s brainchild: a tool designed to simplify precision, speed up execution, and support the next generation of artists, VJs, designers, content curators, and projection-mapping professionals across the world.

For KCee, technology is never just a tool or machinery. It is a creative language that gives one a way to turn music, architecture, light, movement, and emotion into immersive experiences that people carry with them long after they leave.
